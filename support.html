Returns and Refunds

  1. Eligibility:
    • Return products within 30 days of receipt.
    • Products must be unused and in their original packaging.

  2. Initiate a Return:
    • Contact our customer support team for a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.

  3. Prepare the Return:
    • Securely package the item with the original receipt.
    • Ship the package to the address provided by our support team.

  4. Inspection and Refund:
    • Once received, we will inspect the returned item.
    • You will be notified of the approval or rejection of your refund.
    • If approved, a credit will be applied to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.
